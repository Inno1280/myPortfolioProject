The Best Movies Innocent have seen in 2023

Below are a list of my best movies I have seen in the past year.

Gang of Lagos

This movie is a riveting film set in the bustling city of Lagos, Nigeria, where a charismatic leader forms an unconventional alliance known as the Gang of Lagos. Comprising a diverse group of individuals with a shared desire for power and justice, the gang navigates the city's underworld, facing challenges from law enforcement and rival factions. Against the backdrop of vibrant street life and pulsating Afrobeat rhythms, the movie explores themes of loyalty, betrayal, and the relentless pursuit of dreams. As the gang rises to prominence, they must confront their own demons and make daring choices in this gripping tale of ambition and survival in Africa's most populous city.

The Set Up

The movie is a gripping Nigerian thriller that follows the life of Grace, a young woman with dreams of a better life. When she inadvertently becomes entangled in a dangerous world of crime and deceit, Grace finds herself caught in a web of betrayal and manipulation. As she navigates through the complexities of her new reality, Grace must summon inner strength and resilience to outsmart those who seek to exploit her. With unexpected twists and suspenseful turns, "The Setup" explores themes of survival, empowerment, and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

The Mission Impossible: Rogue Nation

This interesting movie follows Ethan Hunt (played by Tom Cruise) and his team as they face their most formidable adversary yet, the Syndicate, a secret organization committed to destabilizing the world order. When the IMF (Impossible Mission Force) is disbanded, Hunt goes rogue to uncover the Syndicate's true agenda. Alongside his trusted allies, including Benji Dunn (Simon Pegg) and Luther Stickell (Ving Rhames), Hunt races against time to expose the Syndicate's leader, Solomon Lane (Sean Harris), and prevent a global catastrophe. Filled with intense action sequences, daring stunts, and unexpected alliances, the film keeps audiences on the edge of their seats as Hunt navigates a web of deceit, betrayal, and high-stakes espionage.
